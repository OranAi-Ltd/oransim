## Model Details

- **Model**: LightGBM Quantile Regression (three quantiles: P35 / P50 / P65)

## Intended Use

- **Primary**: Predict marketing campaign KPIs (impressions, clicks, conversions, ROI) given creative, budget, platform allocation, and KOL mix
- **Out-of-scope**: Fraud detection, ad fraud classification, click-fraud forensics
- **Primary users**: Developers building causal marketing digital twins on the Oransim framework

## Training Data

- **Source**: Synthetic data generated by `scripts/gen_synthetic_data.py` (v0.2)
- **Size**: 100k samples
- **Generating process**: see [`data_card.md`](data_card.md) (coming with v0.2)
- **Note**: Synthetic data is distribution-matched to real Chinese marketing campaigns but does **not** contain any real KOL, note, or user information.

## Limitations

1. **Synthetic-data provenance** — numbers above are not generalized to real-world performance. Validate on your own data before production use.
2. **Chinese market skew** — XHS reference data; may underperform on Western platform distributions without re-calibration.
3. **30% CI bands** — P35–P65 do not cover extreme tail events.
4. **No multimodal** — v0.2 model is text + tabular only; image/video creative understanding lands with v0.5 VLM integration.

## Bias & Fairness

- Training synthetic data is IPF-calibrated on Chinese demographic priors → predictions reflect Chinese-market behavior; apply caution for global markets
- Soul agent personas default to Chinese-language prompts (English personas on v0.5 roadmap)
- No explicit fairness audit performed for v0.1-v0.2; fairness eval on v1.0 roadmap

## Ethical Considerations

- Oransim predicts *likely* campaign outcomes — it is a decision-support tool, not automation. Human marketers should always validate before budget commitment.
- Soul agent feedback is LLM-generated, not real user feedback. Treat it as a complement to, not a replacement for, real focus groups.
